# Curriculum

The full 6-week foundations program. Each week feeds into the next — don't skip ahead.

## Week 1 — Threats & Principles
- **Mon–Tue:** Cisco "Introduction to Cybersecurity" Modules 1–2
- **Wed–Thu:** Professor Messer SY0-701 — Threat actors, vectors, malware
- **Fri:** Professor Messer SY0-701 — Vulnerabilities
- **Weekend:** 20-question quiz + "attack types" cheat sheet

## Week 2 — Networking for Security
- **Mon–Tue:** OSI model + TCP/IP (NetworkChuck or Professor Messer)
- **Wed:** Ports & protocols
- **Thu:** Firewalls, VPNs, proxies, VLANs, subnets
- **Fri:** Subnetting practice (10–15 problems)
- **Weekend:** Network topology diagram in draw.io

## Week 3 — Identity & Access Management
- **Mon–Tue:** Authentication factors & MFA
- **Wed:** SSO & federation (SAML, OAuth, OIDC)
- **Thu:** Authorization models (DAC, MAC, RBAC, ABAC)
- **Fri:** Account lifecycle + PAM
- **Weekend:** Enable MFA on personal accounts + diagram OIDC flow

## Week 4 — Cryptography
- **Mon–Tue:** Symmetric/asymmetric encryption + hashing
- **Wed:** PKI deep dive
- **Thu:** TLS handshake (1.2 vs 1.3)
- **Fri:** Crypto attacks + post-quantum outlook
- **Weekend:** Inspect a real cert chain + draw TLS handshake from memory
